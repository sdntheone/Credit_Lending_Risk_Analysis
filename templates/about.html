About the Credit Approval System

How It Works

The Credit Approval System uses machine learning to predict the appropriate customer segment (P1, P2, P3, or P4) based on the applicant's financial behavior, credit history, and personal profile.

Key Features

  • Displays confidence score for predictions
  • Ensures transparency of decision factors

The Model

Trained on extensive historical data, the model evaluates trends in:

  • Payment history and delinquencies
  • Active and closed trade lines
  • Monthly income and employment duration
  • Demographics like education and marital status
  • Recent credit applications and inquiries

Data Privacy

All submitted data is encrypted and used only for generating predictions. We adhere to strict confidentiality protocols and do not retain any personal data after processing.

Disclaimer
